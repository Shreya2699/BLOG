Maggi in white sauce

Ready for some yummy Ozzy cheesy recipes? There you go.....a yummy cheesy comfort recipes to soothe your mood any time. It especially feels great during chilled winter days or rainy days. So in this monsoon season get ready for some drooling cheesy recipes-maggi in white sauce

ingredient-

cheddar cheese/processed cheese
carrot (finely chopped)
capsicum(finely chopped)
milk half cup
maggie-1 packet
jeera
black pepper

procedure-

Add 1 tablespoon oil in a pan. Add jeera to it and saute. Add carrot and capsicum and saute. Add water to it maggie such that it's just enough to boil it and doesn't leave any extra water. Add milk and cheese to it and boil it. Add salt and black pepper to it.Add the maggie masala or tastemaker. Serve hot!
